Our Story

Casa di Roma was founded in 1995 by the Romano family, bringing authentic Italian flavors to our city. Our passion for traditional Italian cuisine drives us to source the finest ingredients directly from Italy.

Our chefs have trained in the best culinary schools of Italy and bring decades of experience in creating authentic Italian dishes that transport you straight to the streets of Rome, Naples, and Milan.
